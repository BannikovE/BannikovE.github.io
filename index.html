ABOUT ME

I'm Egor Bannikov, PhD student and researcher in elementary particle and high energy physics.

I have studied nuclear physics at St. Petersburg Polytechnic University (SPbPU) under the bachelor and master. Now I am the PhD student of elementary particle department.

During my study, I joined the PHENIX collaboration at RHIC collider. My research is devoted to the elliptic flow for \(\pi^0\) in Cu+Au collisions at \(\sqrt{s_{_{NN}}}\rm{=200~ GeV}\).
